## What is an AI receptionist?

An AI receptionist is an automated voice assistant that answers incoming calls, understands what the caller wants, and handles the request — without human involvement for routine matters.

For restaurant use, that means concretely:

- Takes orders (with menu questions, allergies, delivery address)
- Manages table bookings
- Answers opening-hours and menu questions
- Sends confirmations via SMS
- Escalates to a real person when needed

Technically: the AI uses **speech-to-text** to understand voice, a **language model** (like GPT-4 or Claude) to interpret intent, and **text-to-speech** to respond with a natural voice. The whole conversation happens in real-time with 1-2 second latency.

## What can an AI receptionist do (and not do)?

### What it handles well

**Structured tasks** with a clear flow:
- Orders with standard menu items
- Table bookings (date, time, party size)
- Hours and contact questions
- Standardized FAQs ("do you have gluten-free?", "can I cancel?")

**Volume** — can take 50+ simultaneous calls without quality drop.

**Consistent tone** — never says wrong price, never forgets the allergy question, always confirms delivery address.

### What it shouldn't handle

**Complex complaints** — if the customer is angry or has a specific situation, escalate directly to a human.

**Crisis situations** — e.g. food poisoning, allergic reaction. The AI should detect keywords and transfer immediately.

**Negotiations** — discounts, special arrangements. That's still human work.

## What's required to implement?

We typically split it into 3 phases:

### Phase 1: Preparation (1-2 weeks)

- Define **scope** — which calls should the AI handle?
- Compile the **menu** in structured form (prices, ingredients, variants)
- List **business rules** (hours, delivery area, payment methods)
- Decide on **voice** (female/male, accent, tone)

### Phase 2: Build + training (2-3 weeks)

- We build the AI with your specific data
- **Test calls** — we simulate common and edge-case scenarios
- Iteration: listen to test calls, adjust responses, test again
- Integration with your **POS system** (Square, Lightspeed, etc.) so orders are created automatically

### Phase 3: Launch + fine-tuning (1-2 weeks)

- **Soft launch** — AI takes 30% of calls, you hear the rest
- **A/B testing** — compare AI vs staff responses for quality
- **Escalation flow** — when AI doesn't know, it transfers to you
- **Live mode** — AI handles 100% of routine calls

**Total time-to-live:** 4-7 weeks from start to production.

## Common objections — and answers

**"My customers will hate talking to a robot."**

Modern AI no longer sounds robotic. Sannegården customers often don't notice they're talking to an AI. Plus: they appreciate always getting an answer versus leaving voicemail or being put on hold.

**"What if the AI says the wrong thing?"**

Good question. We build in **confidence thresholds** — if the AI is uncertain (e.g. doesn't hear clearly or the customer asks something unusual), it transfers directly to you. Plus: all calls are recorded (with customer consent) so you can audit quality.

**"Do we need to switch our POS system?"**

No. The AI integrates with existing systems via APIs. We have experience with Square, Lightspeed, Caspeco, and custom integrations to legacy systems.
